How it works

  • Automated scanning — pages are checked against Google Safe Browsing, urlscan.io, and domain registration age.
  • Smart heuristics — local analysis catches impersonated brands, bait-word domains, and suspicious TLDs, even offline.
  • Community reports — users flag scam sites in one tap; verified reports protect every ScamGuard user worldwide.

Privacy first

Only the domain name of the current page is ever sent to our servers — not the full URL, not your identity, not your browsing history. The backend is open source, so you can verify that yourself. Read the full privacy policy.

ScamGuard's backend is open source (MIT) — the community data belongs to the community.
